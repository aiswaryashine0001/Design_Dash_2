About
FISAT and Impulse 5.0

FISAT (Federal Institute of Science and Technology) is a premier engineering institution located in Mookkannoor, Kerala, India. Established in 2002, FISAT has been a beacon of technical education and innovation, offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering and technology disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to academic excellence, FISAT continues to be a leading institution in shaping the future of engineering education.

Impulse 5.0 is the annual technical fest organized by FISAT, bringing together students, professionals, and enthusiasts from various fields to showcase their talents and innovations. This year's event, Impulse 5.0, promises to be bigger and better than ever, featuring a range of exciting activities, including workshops, seminars, competitions, and exhibitions. With a focus on cutting-edge technology and creative problem-solving, Impulse 5.0 provides a platform for participants to engage with industry experts, explore new ideas, and network with peers.

The event will be held at the FISAT campus on [Event Date], and it will offer a unique opportunity for attendees to immerse themselves in the latest trends and developments in technology. Join us for an exhilarating experience at Impulse 5.0 and be a part of this remarkable celebration of innovation and excellence!
